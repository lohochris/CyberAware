About This Website

An academic cyberbullying awareness platform designed to support adults in England through education, guidance, and ethical signposting.

Purpose of the Project

This website was developed as part of an academic research project aimed at improving awareness of cyberbullying and online harassment among adults in England. The platform provides educational information, practical guidance, and signposting to support services.

Ethical Considerations

This website does not collect personal information, does not store user responses, and does not provide counselling or mental health diagnosis. All content is intended strictly for educational and awareness purposes.

Academic Context

The project was conducted within the School of Science and Technology, Nottingham Trent University, Nottingham, UK, and adheres to established research ethics and data protection principles.
