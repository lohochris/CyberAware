Support and Help

If online abuse or harassment is affecting your wellbeing, support is available. The organisations listed below provide confidential advice and assistance. This website does not provide counselling or emergency services.

If you are in immediate danger:

Call 999 for emergency assistance.

Samaritans (UK)

Emotional support for anyone experiencing distress or feeling overwhelmed. Available 24 hours a day.

Phone: 116 123

Visit website

NHS 111

Get urgent health advice when you are not sure what to do.

Phone: 111

Visit website

UK Safer Internet Centre

Advice on staying safe online and reporting harmful content.

Visit website
