Privacy and Data Use

Your privacy is important. This page explains how information is handled when you use this website.

Personal Data Collection

This website does not collect, store, or process any personal data. Users are not required to create accounts, provide names, email addresses, or submit personal information to access content.

User Interactions

Interactive features, such as awareness scenarios and quizzes, operate entirely within your browser. No responses, scores, or inputs are stored or transmitted to the server.

Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies, analytics tools, or third-party tracking technologies.

External Links

This website includes links to external support organisations. Once you leave this website, their privacy policies will apply.

Legal and Ethical Basis

The website has been designed in accordance with UK data protection principles and academic research ethics guidelines. All content is provided for educational and awareness purposes only.
